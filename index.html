1. How does your legal service work?

Our platform connects you with experienced advocates who specialize in a wide range of legal fields. Once you submit your case details, we match you with the best advocate for your specific needs. The advocate will review your case, provide legal advice, and represent you in court if necessary.

2. What types of legal cases do you handle?

We handle a variety of legal cases including civil litigation, criminal defense, family law (divorce, custody), property disputes, corporate law, personal injury, and more. Whether your case is straightforward or complex, we have advocates experienced in different legal areas to assist you.

3. How do I hire an advocate through your platform?

To hire an advocate, simply submit your case details through our website. After reviewing your submission, we’ll connect you with a qualified advocate. You can communicate directly with your assigned advocate, discuss your case, and proceed with legal action or court representation.

4. What are the costs involved in hiring an advocate?

The costs depend on the complexity of your case and the level of representation required. Our advocates offer competitive rates, and we provide transparent pricing from the start. Some cases may also be eligible for flat fees, while others may follow hourly rates. You can discuss pricing with your advocate before proceeding.
